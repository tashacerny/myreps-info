summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill invokes the War Powers Resolution to direct the President to remove U.S. Armed Forces from any ongoing or imminent hostilities involving Iran. The War Powers Resolution, enacted in 1973, allows Congress to require the President to terminate military operations in cases where Congress has not authorized military action. This bill would use that mechanism to end U.S. military involvement in hostilities with Iran.  The bill would affect U.S. military personnel and operations currently engaged in or preparing for military action against Iran or Iranian forces. It would also impact the scope of presidential authority to conduct military operations in this context, requiring compliance with the terms of the War Powers Resolution regarding Congressional oversight of military commitments.  Key elements of such a measure typically include: directing the removal of armed forces within a specified timeframe, potentially with exceptions for operations necessary to protect American personnel or facilities, and reaffirming Congress's constitutional role in authorizing military action.
