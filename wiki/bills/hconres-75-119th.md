summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill invokes the War Powers Resolution, a 1973 law that allows Congress to direct the President to withdraw U.S. armed forces from military hostilities. Specifically, it directs the President to remove U.S. Armed Forces from any ongoing or anticipated hostilities against Iran. The War Powers Resolution requires the President to notify Congress within 48 hours of committing armed forces to military action and prohibits armed forces from remaining in hostilities for more than 60 days without congressional authorization.  The bill would affect all U.S. military personnel engaged in hostilities against Iran, including those in the Middle East and surrounding regions. It would also impact any military operations, strikes, or defensive actions that the administration might be planning against Iranian targets or forces.  This type of resolution is a congressional check on executive power. It does not authorize military action but rather restricts it, requiring withdrawal unless Congress votes to approve continued military engagement. The bill's passage would require both chambers of Congress to approve the directive."
