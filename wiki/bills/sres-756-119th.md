The resolution would affect public awareness efforts and observances related to gun violence. It may encourage organizations, institutions, and communities to hold events or activities during the designated day and month to increase public awareness of gun violence as an issue. The resolution does not impose requirements on any individuals or organizations, nor does it allocate funding or establish new policies.  Key aspects include the formal recognition of specific dates for awareness purposes and implicit support for drawing national attention to gun violence during June 2026. As a resolution rather than legislation, this measure expresses the sense of Congress but carries no binding legal force or budgetary implications."
