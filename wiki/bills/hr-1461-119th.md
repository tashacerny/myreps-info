summary: "# Bill Summary: Mary Elizabeth Bettie Cole Post Office Building Designation  This bill proposes to officially designate the United States Postal Service facility located at 521 Thorn Street in Sewickley, Pennsylvania, as the \"Mary Elizabeth Bettie Cole Post Office Building.\" The designation is a naming honor that does not affect the facility's operations or postal services.  The bill primarily affects the Sewickley, Pennsylvania community and the USPS facility serving that area. By giving the post office building an official name, it creates a formal recognition that will appear on official materials and signage associated with the location.  This type of legislation is a common congressional action that honors individuals by designating federal buildings in their name. The bill contains no provisions related to funding, facility modifications, or changes to USPS operations—it is purely a naming designation."
