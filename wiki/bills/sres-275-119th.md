summary: "# Summary of Resolution Honoring Pulse Nightclub Attack Victims  This resolution commemorates the victims of the mass shooting that occurred at the Pulse nightclub in Orlando, Florida, on June 12, 2016. The attack resulted in 49 deaths and numerous injuries. The resolution serves as a formal statement by the legislative body to honor those who were killed and acknowledge the impact of the tragedy on survivors, families, and the community.  As a resolution rather than legislation, this measure does not create new laws or programs. Instead, it expresses the official recognition and respect of the legislature for the victims and those affected by the attack. Resolutions of this type typically affirm shared values and allow lawmakers to make formal statements about significant national events. This resolution would affect no specific groups through policy changes but serves a commemorative purpose for the broader public record."
