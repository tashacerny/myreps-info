summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill would direct the Assistant Attorney General to provide grant funding to the National Police Athletic/Activities League (PAL), an organization that operates youth programs in partnership with law enforcement agencies. The grants would support PAL's existing activities and operations.  The bill targets youth in communities served by PAL, aiming to improve academic performance, social development, and overall well-being. The programs would be conducted by law enforcement personnel outside of school hours and are intended to reduce juvenile crime rates and prevent youth from becoming crime victims by engaging young people in structured, productive activities.  Key provisions include: federal grant funding administered by the Assistant Attorney General for the Department of Justice; support for youth programs run jointly by PAL and local law enforcement; and a focus on after-school and non-school hour activities designed to provide mentorship, recreational opportunities, and academic support."
