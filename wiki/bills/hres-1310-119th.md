summary: "# Bill Summary  This resolution expresses congressional support for efforts to combat fraud, waste, abuse, and improper payments in Medicare, Medicaid, and other federal health care programs. The bill advocates for strengthened program integrity measures, enhanced oversight, and coordinated enforcement actions to protect these systems. It does not propose new legislation or establish new requirements; rather, it is a statement of support for continued anti-fraud and abuse initiatives within existing federal health care programs.  The resolution affects federal health care programs and their beneficiaries, as well as federal agencies responsible for program administration and enforcement, including the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, the Department of Health and Human Services Office of Inspector General, and the Department of Justice. The bill recognizes investigative and prosecutorial efforts aimed at identifying and addressing improper use of program funds.  The key focus areas include program integrity measures to detect and prevent fraud, strengthened oversight mechanisms to monitor program spending, and coordinated enforcement actions across agencies to investigate and prosecute violations. The resolution frames these efforts as important to protecting taxpayer resources and maintaining the long-term financial viability of the nation's federal health care safety net programs."
