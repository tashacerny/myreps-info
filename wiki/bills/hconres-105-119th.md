summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill would direct the President to remove United States Armed Forces from any active hostilities involving Iran, invoking the War Powers Resolution. The War Powers Resolution is a 1973 law that requires the President to notify Congress within 48 hours of committing armed forces to military action and mandates the removal of those forces within 60 days unless Congress authorizes their continued involvement. This bill uses Section 5(c) of that law, which allows Congress to direct the President to withdraw armed forces engaged in hostilities.  The bill would affect all U.S. military personnel involved in combat or active military operations against Iran or Iranian forces. It would apply to ongoing military operations and would require the President to cease such hostilities and withdraw relevant armed forces. The measure does not appear to address military presence in the region more broadly—only active armed conflict with Iran specifically.  **Note:** Without the full bill text, this summary reflects the general scope indicated by the title.
