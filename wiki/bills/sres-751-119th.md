summary: "# Summary of ALS Awareness Month Resolution  This resolution designates May 2026 as \"ALS Awareness Month\" at the federal level. The measure is a symbolic action that recognizes amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS), a progressive neurodegenerative disease, and aims to increase public awareness about the condition during that month.  The resolution does not create new programs, funding, or substantive policy changes. It affects no specific individuals or organizations directly, though it may benefit ALS advocacy groups and patient organizations by providing an official platform to raise awareness. The designation encourages educational efforts and recognition of the disease and those living with it during May 2026.  This type of resolution is a common legislative tool used to highlight health conditions, diseases, and causes of public concern. It serves primarily to draw attention to the issue rather than to establish legal requirements or allocate resources."
