Grant Post Office Building.\" The designation is a naming honor for the historic facility, which serves the Georgetown community.  The bill affects the USPS facility in Georgetown and has no direct operational or budgetary impact. It is a ceremonial measure that recognizes the historical connection between the post office's location and Ulysses S. Grant, who was born in Georgetown, Ohio. Once enacted, the facility would be officially referred to by this designated name in federal records and usage."
