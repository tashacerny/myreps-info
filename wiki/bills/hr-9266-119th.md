Placards are standardized warning labels that identify the type of hazard present (such as flammable, toxic, or corrosive materials). The bill aims to ensure that hazardous materials in refrigerated containers—which may be used for temperature-sensitive shipments—are clearly marked during transport.  The regulations would primarily affect companies that transport hazardous materials using refrigerated containers, including manufacturers, shipping companies, and logistics providers. It would also apply to workers and emergency responders who may encounter these shipments. The requirement would standardize safety communication across the transportation industry by ensuring that all refrigerated containers carrying hazardous materials display appropriate warning placards, similar to existing requirements for other types of shipping containers.
