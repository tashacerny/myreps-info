summary: "# Summary of Osteoporosis Prevention and Awareness Month Resolution  This resolution expresses congressional support for designating May 2026 as \"Osteoporosis Prevention and Awareness Month.\" The measure does not create any binding requirements or allocate funding; rather, it is a symbolic statement intended to raise public awareness about osteoporosis and the importance of prevention and early detection.  The resolution affects public health messaging and outreach efforts by encouraging federal, state, and local health organizations, healthcare providers, and community groups to use the designated month to educate the public about osteoporosis risk factors, screening options, and prevention strategies. This designation may prompt increased media coverage, public health campaigns, and educational activities related to bone health during May 2026.  While the resolution carries no legal force, such designations typically serve to highlight health issues and mobilize awareness efforts among medical professionals and the general public. Osteoporosis is a condition affecting bone density that can increase fracture risk, particularly in older adults and postmenopausal women."
