summary: "# Summary: A Resolution Recognizing the 175th Anniversary of Alpha Delta Pi Sorority  This resolution commemorates the 175th anniversary of Alpha Delta Pi Sorority's founding. The measure recognizes the organization's history and acknowledges its establishment date. As a resolution rather than legislation, this measure does not create new laws or direct government action; it serves as an expression of recognition by the legislative body.  Alpha Delta Pi is a women's social sorority with chapters at colleges and universities across the United States. The resolution affects members of the sorority, chapter communities, and the organization itself by formally recognizing this organizational milestone. The measure would express the legislature's acknowledgment of the sorority's longevity and its role in campus and community life."
