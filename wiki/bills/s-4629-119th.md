summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill would prohibit the federal government from providing financial assistance to state and local governments and school districts to help them meet their existing financial obligations or debts. It would also restrict the Federal Reserve banks and the Department of the Treasury from offering financial assistance to these entities if they have defaulted on their obligations. The bill applies broadly to states, municipalities, and school districts across the country.  The legislation would affect state governments, cities and counties, and public school systems that might otherwise seek federal funding to address budget shortfalls or debt problems. It would limit the federal financial tools currently available to help these entities manage fiscal crises or meet their debt payments.  The bill's stated purpose is to prevent federal funds from being used to bail out or financially assist state and local governments and school districts, essentially requiring these entities to manage their own financial obligations without direct federal rescue funding."
