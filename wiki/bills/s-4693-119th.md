summary: "# Bill Summary: Hybrid Space Architecture Pilot Program  This bill directs the Secretary of Defense to establish and conduct an operational pilot program focused on the Hybrid Space Architecture (HSA) initiative. The program would evaluate whether the Department of Defense can effectively use commercially available orbital data center services and space-based cloud computing capabilities to meet national security and military operational needs. In practical terms, this means testing whether private-sector space-based computing infrastructure can support defense and military functions.  The bill affects the Department of Defense, commercial space service providers, and potentially military operations that depend on computing and data processing. By running a pilot program rather than full implementation, the Defense Department would assess the technical feasibility, security implications, and operational effectiveness of relying on commercial space-based computing before making broader commitments.  Key provisions would likely include establishing pilot program parameters, defining evaluation metrics for success, assessing compatibility with existing military systems, and determining security and reliability standards for space-based cloud services in defense contexts. The \"for other purposes\" clause suggests the bill may also include related administrative or technical provisions supporting this initiative."
