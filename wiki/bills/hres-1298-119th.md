summary: "# Summary of National Charter Schools Week Resolution  This bill designates May 10–16, 2026, as \"National Charter Schools Week\" in the United States. The resolution supports recognition and celebration of charter schools and the principles they represent during this designated period.  The bill affects no government programs, funding, or regulations. It is a symbolic measure that calls for observance of the week but does not create any new requirements or changes to law. Charter schools—which are publicly funded but independently operated schools typically freed from some standard regulations in exchange for accountability measures—would be the focus of recognition during this week.  The resolution may be used to encourage awareness of charter schools, highlight their achievements, and celebrate stakeholders including students, teachers, and administrators involved in charter school education."
