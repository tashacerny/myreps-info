summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill invokes the War Powers Resolution to direct the President to remove U.S. Armed Forces from any hostilities involving Iran. The War Powers Resolution, enacted in 1973, allows Congress to require the President to withdraw military forces from conflicts without a formal declaration of war if Congress passes such a measure. This bill would use that mechanism to mandate the withdrawal of U.S. forces engaged in hostilities with Iran.  The bill would affect U.S. military personnel currently involved in operations against Iran or Iranian forces. It applies to armed forces under presidential command in any capacity related to hostilities with Iran, though the specific scope would depend on how \"hostilities\" is defined in the full text and interpreted by the administration.  Key provisions would include a requirement for the President to cease U.S. military operations against Iran and withdraw armed forces from such hostilities. Depending on the bill's language, it may include a timeline for withdrawal, exemptions for certain operations (such as defensive actions), or additional reporting requirements to Congress about the withdrawal process."
