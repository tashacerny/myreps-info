The designation is intended to promote awareness about children's health and safety related to firearm storage practices in homes. The bill does not create new laws or impose requirements; rather, it is a symbolic measure endorsing a specific day for public awareness and education purposes.  The bill affects child safety advocacy organizations, parents, and the general public by encouraging promotion of safe firearm storage practices. The measure would bring attention to the ASK initiative, which focuses on encouraging safe conversations between parents and caregivers about gun storage in households where children may be present. National designation days typically serve to raise public awareness about specific health, safety, or social causes through media attention and community activities."
