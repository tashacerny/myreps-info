summary: "# Summary of Senate Resolution on Mifepristone  This resolution expresses the Senate's position that mifepristone—a medication used in medication abortion—should be governed by evidence-based science and policy. The resolution references over 25 years of real-world evidence and hundreds of peer-reviewed studies regarding the safety and effectiveness of the drug, and calls for law and policy on medication abortion access to be equitable, transparent, and grounded in scientific evidence.  The resolution affects policy related to medication abortion access across the United States. It does not propose new legislation or create legal requirements, but rather states a position intended to influence how lawmakers and policymakers approach regulations governing this medication.  Key to the resolution is its call for policies on mifepristone access to be based on peer-reviewed scientific evidence rather than other considerations, and for such policies to be equitable and transparent. By framing medication abortion as \"lifesaving, time-sensitive\" care, the resolution characterizes this as a healthcare access issue that should align with medical evidence."
