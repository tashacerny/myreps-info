summary: "# Summary: National Brain Tumor Awareness Month Resolution  This resolution proposes to designate May 2026 as \"National Brain Tumor Awareness Month.\" The resolution is a symbolic measure that recognizes and raises awareness about brain tumors as a public health matter. It does not create new programs, funding, or regulatory requirements.  The resolution affects the federal government's official calendar and messaging. It encourages federal agencies, organizations, and the public to acknowledge brain tumor awareness during the designated month through education, outreach, and recognition activities. The designation may benefit brain tumor patients, survivors, caregivers, medical researchers, and advocacy organizations by providing a nationally recognized platform to highlight the condition.  As a congressional resolution, this measure does not appropriate funds or carry the force of law. Its primary effect is to establish an official observance that can guide federal communications and encourage public and private sector awareness efforts related to brain tumors."
