The resolution applies broadly to attacks motivated by antisemitic ideology across the country, not limited to a specific region or incident.  Key elements of the measure include: condemnation of ideologically motivated attacks on Jewish individuals; acknowledgment of a specific violent incident in Boulder, Colorado; and an affirmation of the House's commitment to combating both antisemitism and violence motivated by political ideology.
