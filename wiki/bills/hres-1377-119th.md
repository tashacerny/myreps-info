summary: "# Summary of House Consideration Resolution  This resolution is a procedural measure that establishes rules for floor consideration of four separate bills in the House of Representatives. It does not create new policy itself but rather determines how these four bills will be debated and voted upon.  The four bills addressed are: (1) **H.R. 1181**, which would prohibit payment card networks and covered entities from creating or requiring merchant category codes that specifically identify firearms retailers separately from other retail categories; (2) **H.R. 9022**, an appropriations bill funding energy and water development agencies for fiscal year 2027; (3) **H.R. 8595**, an appropriations bill funding national security, the Department of State, and related programs for fiscal year 2027; and (4) **H.R. 9237**, legislation to expand veterans' benefits and improve Department of Veterans Affairs administration.  By approving this resolution, the House would allow these bills to proceed to debate and amendment on the chamber floor. The resolution sets the procedural framework—such as time limits for debate and which amendments may be offered—but the substantive outcomes depend on separate votes on each bill itself."
