summary: "# Bill Summary: Lower Presidio Historic Park Resource Study  This bill directs the U.S. Secretary of the Interior to conduct a special resource study of Lower Presidio Historic Park in Monterey, California. A special resource study is a formal evaluation process used to assess whether a site meets criteria for designation as a unit of the National Park System, or to evaluate its suitability for other forms of federal protection or management. The study would examine the park's historical significance, resource conditions, and potential management approaches.  The study would primarily affect the Lower Presidio Historic Park site and the Monterey area. The Secretary of the Interior, through the National Park Service, would be responsible for completing the evaluation and reporting findings. The results could inform future decisions about the site's federal status and preservation priorities.  This type of legislation is procedural in nature and does not immediately establish new protections or change existing management of the site. Rather, it initiates an investigative process that could lead to recommendations for future action by Congress or the federal government regarding how the historic park should be managed or protected."
