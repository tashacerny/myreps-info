summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill would prevent the U.S. Postal Service (USPS) from finalizing, implementing, or enforcing a proposed rule that would restrict or regulate the mailing of certain firearms, specifically pistols, revolvers, and other concealable weapons. The bill directly blocks regulatory action on this issue, effectively maintaining current USPS policies regarding firearm shipments.  The bill would affect the USPS, firearms manufacturers, licensed firearms dealers, and individuals who mail firearms through the postal system. It would also impact the regulatory process by preventing the agency from moving forward with new rules on this topic.  The key provision is a broad prohibition on the USPS taking any action to finalize, implement, or enforce the proposed firearms mailing rule. The bill does not establish new mailing procedures or create alternative regulations—it solely blocks the USPS from implementing changes to current practice regarding concealable firearms shipments."
