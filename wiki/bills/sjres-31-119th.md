summary: "# Bill Summary  This joint resolution seeks to overturn an Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) rule through the Congressional Review Act (CRA), a legal mechanism that allows Congress to disapprove of federal agency regulations. The specific rule in question involves EPA's reclassification of certain industrial facilities from \"major sources\" to \"area sources\" under Section 112 of the Clean Air Act. Major sources are facilities that emit or have the potential to emit specified quantities of hazardous air pollutants and face stricter regulatory requirements, while area sources are subject to less stringent standards.  The rule affects industrial facilities that would be reclassified downward under the EPA's decision, as well as the agencies responsible for enforcing air quality standards. If passed, this joint resolution would invalidate the EPA's reclassification rule and prevent the agency from issuing substantially the same rule without further Congressional action. The practical effect would be that facilities covered by the original rule would retain their \"major source\" classification and remain subject to the associated regulatory requirements and compliance obligations."
