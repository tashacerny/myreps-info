summary: "# Summary of Taiwanese American Heritage Week Bill  This bill proposes to officially designate the week of May 10–16, 2026, as \"Taiwanese American Heritage Week\" at the federal level. The designation would recognize and honor the history, culture, and contributions of Taiwanese Americans to the United States. Such designations are symbolic measures that draw national attention to specific communities and their cultural significance, though they do not create new programs or funding.  The bill affects Taiwanese Americans and organizations representing this community, as well as the broader American public by acknowledging this ethnic and cultural group. The designation may encourage educational activities, cultural events, and public recognition during that week. Congress frequently passes similar resolutions designating heritage weeks for various ethnic, cultural, and historical groups throughout the year. The primary impact is to provide official congressional recognition rather than to establish legal requirements or allocate resources."
