The measure reaffirms the importance of immigrants from all backgrounds to the United States and calls on all elected officials to avoid using language that promotes racial or ethnic division. The bill does not propose new laws or regulations; rather, it is a statement of Congressional position on these issues.  The bill affects all Americans, particularly Indian and Chinese Americans who may be targets of discriminatory language or rhetoric. It also addresses elected officials at all levels of government, urging them to refrain from divisive speech based on race or ethnicity. The measure is symbolic in nature, intended to send a policy message rather than establish legal requirements or create enforcement mechanisms."
