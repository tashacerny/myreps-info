Rather than imposing mandatory requirements, the legislation creates a framework for businesses to participate in sharing details about how AI is deployed in products and services that affect consumers.  **Who It Affects**  The bill primarily affects businesses that develop or deploy AI systems in consumer-facing applications, as well as consumers who interact with these systems. Companies participating in the pilot would voluntarily agree to disclosure practices, while consumers would gain access to information about AI use in the products or services they purchase or use.  **Key Provisions**  While the full text is unavailable, the pilot structure typically includes: establishment of disclosure standards or guidelines that participating companies would follow; a defined timeframe for the pilot program; and mechanisms for tracking participation and outcomes.
