summary: "# Summary of House Rules Resolution  This resolution sets floor procedures for consideration of four separate bills in the House of Representatives. It establishes the terms under which debate and votes will occur on each measure, including H.R. 4016 (Defense Department appropriations for fiscal year 2026), H.R. 3633 (digital commodities regulation by the SEC and CFTC), H.R. 1919 (restrictions on Federal Reserve services and central bank digital currency), and S. 1582 (stablecoin payment regulation). The resolution also waives a procedural requirement under House Rule XIII related to certain resolutions from the Committee on Rules.  The resolution affects Members of Congress and, depending on the underlying bills' provisions, would impact the Department of Defense, financial regulators, the Federal Reserve, and the cryptocurrency and digital asset industries. It is primarily procedural in nature—determining how and when these bills will be debated and voted on rather than establishing substantive policy itself."
