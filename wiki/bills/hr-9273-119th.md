Medical schools would need to incorporate menopause education into their existing curricula, though the bill does not specify the depth, duration, or specific content requirements based on the title alone.  The bill addresses medical education standards by directing federal requirements for institutions that receive federal funding. It would affect medical schools, their students, and potentially patients who receive care from physicians trained under these new standards.
