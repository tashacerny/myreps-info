summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill proposes to rename a playground located within New River Gorge National Park and Preserve in West Virginia. The new name would be \"Hearts of Gold Playground: In Honor of West Virginia Children and Families Impacted by Childhood Cancer.\" The redesignation is a commemorative action intended to honor children and families in the state who have been affected by childhood cancer.  The bill affects the National Park Service, which manages the national park and preserve, and would require updating signage and official records to reflect the new playground name. The change is primarily symbolic and does not appear to involve funding, operational changes, or alterations to the playground itself."
