The Ready Reserve Force is a fleet of government-owned ships maintained by the Maritime Administration that can be activated during national emergencies or when commercial shipping capacity is insufficient to meet national defense needs. The bill emphasizes expediting the design phase for new vessels that would be added to this fleet.  **Who It Affects**  The bill primarily affects the Maritime Administration and the Department of Transportation, which oversee the Ready Reserve Force. It may also impact the shipping industry, defense and logistics operations that depend on surge shipping capacity, and shipyards that would potentially construct these vessels. Federal agencies involved in maritime operations and national defense logistics would need to prioritize these design requirements.  **Key Provisions**  While the full text is unavailable, the bill's title indicates it focuses on accelerating the finalization of design specifications for RRF vessels.
