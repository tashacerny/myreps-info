The bill would impose a federal tax on payments distributed from these funds, expanding the taxable events currently covered under federal tax law.  The bill would affect individuals and entities receiving distributions from settlement funds, as well as the settlement fund administrators who manage these accounts. This includes recipients of personal injury settlements, class action lawsuit proceeds, and other legal settlements that are structured through settlement funds.
