summary: "# Bill Summary  This joint resolution seeks to block a regulatory action by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (CFPB) through the congressional disapproval process. Specifically, it targets the CFPB's withdrawal of \"Consumer Financial Protection Circular 2024-03,\" which addressed unlawful and unenforceable contract terms and conditions in consumer financial products and services. If passed, this resolution would prevent the CFPB from withdrawing that circular.  The resolution affects the CFPB's authority to issue guidance and potentially impacts consumers and financial institutions that offer consumer financial products. The circular in question provided guidance to financial companies about what contract terms the CFPB considers unlawful or unenforceable. By disapproving the withdrawal, Congress would be using its authority under the Congressional Review Act (Chapter 8 of Title 5) to overturn a regulatory action, effectively requiring the circular to remain in effect."
