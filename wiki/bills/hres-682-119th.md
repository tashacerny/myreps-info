summary: "# Legislative Summary  This measure is a procedural resolution that establishes the terms for House floor consideration of two separate bills. It does not itself create policy but rather sets the framework for debating and voting on the underlying legislation.  **H.R. 3838** authorizes federal spending for the Department of Defense in fiscal year 2026, covering military operations, military construction projects, and defense-related activities within the Department of Energy. The bill also sets authorized personnel strength levels for the armed forces. This affects active-duty and reserve military personnel, defense contractors, and federal agencies involved in national security operations.  **H.R. 3486** amends immigration law to increase criminal penalties for individuals who illegally enter the United States or re-enter after being formally removed. This would affect immigration enforcement agencies, federal courts handling immigration cases, and individuals subject to deportation proceedings. The resolution allows the House to debate and vote on both bills under specified procedures."
