summary: "# Summary of Wildfire Preparedness Week Resolution  This bill expresses congressional support for designating May 3–9, 2026, as \"Wildfire Preparedness Week,\" a national observance focused on educating the public about fire safety and preparedness. The resolution endorses the goals and purpose of this awareness week, which aims to inform Americans about risks related to wildfires and steps they can take to prepare themselves and their properties.  The bill affects the general public and organizations involved in wildfire prevention and emergency management, including fire departments, emergency response agencies, and community groups. By supporting this designation, Congress would acknowledge the importance of wildfire preparedness as a public safety matter.  Key provisions include the official recognition of the designated week and support for educational activities related to fire safety and preparedness. This is a nonbinding resolution that does not appropriate funds or create new legal requirements; rather, it signals congressional support for a national awareness initiative."
