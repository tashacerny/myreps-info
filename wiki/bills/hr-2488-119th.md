The facility would be officially designated as the \"Grantland Johnson Post Office.\" This type of legislation is a naming designation bill, which honors an individual by attaching their name to a federal building or facility.  The bill directly affects the postal facility and its operations in Sacramento. It also affects anyone who conducts business at that location, as mail addressed to the facility would reference the new name. The change is primarily ceremonial and does not alter the facility's functions, services, or operations.  The key provision is simply the official designation of the name. No other changes to postal services, funding, or operations are proposed. Grantland Johnson is the individual being honored through this designation, though the bill text alone does not specify the reasons for this honor."
