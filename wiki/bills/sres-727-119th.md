summary: "# Summary of Mental Health Awareness in Agriculture Day Resolution  This resolution designates May 29, 2026, as \"Mental Health Awareness in Agriculture Day.\" The measure is intended to draw public and policymaker attention to mental health issues affecting people who work in agriculture, including farmers, farmworkers, and others employed in the agricultural industry.  The resolution aims to accomplish two primary goals: increasing awareness of mental health challenges in the agricultural sector and reducing stigma surrounding mental illness. The designation does not create new programs or funding but rather establishes a symbolic day to highlight these issues. The resolution affects agricultural workers and organizations, mental health advocates, and federal recognition of the cause.  As a resolution rather than a law, this measure does not impose requirements or create enforceable obligations. It serves as an expression of Congress's position on the importance of addressing mental health in agriculture and may encourage related public awareness campaigns, discussions, or voluntary initiatives by agricultural organizations and mental health groups."
