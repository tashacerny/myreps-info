Council legislation within a specified timeframe. This disapproval resolution exercises that congressional power to block the D.C. tax measure from taking effect.  The D.C. tax law at issue appears to involve changes to income and franchise taxes in the District, likely adjusting how D.C. tax code aligns with federal tax law (conformity) and revising various tax provisions. The bill would affect D.C. residents and businesses subject to D.C. income and franchise taxes, as well as the District's tax revenue and budget. However, without the full text, the specific tax changes proposed cannot be detailed here.  If enacted, this disapproval resolution would prevent the D.C.
