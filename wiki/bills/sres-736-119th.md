summary: "# Summary of National Hospital Week Resolution (2026)  This resolution designates May 10–16, 2026, as National Hospital Week and expresses support for the goals and ideals associated with this observance. The resolution is a symbolic measure that does not create new laws or programs; rather, it formally recognizes a specific week dedicated to acknowledging hospitals and their role in the healthcare system.  The resolution affects the general public and healthcare institutions, particularly hospitals. It provides a formal platform to highlight the work of hospital staff, administrators, and the services hospitals provide to their communities. National Hospital Week observances typically focus on recognizing hospital employees, raising awareness about hospital services, and celebrating contributions to public health.  As a resolution, this measure carries no binding requirements or budget implications. It serves as a statement of congressional support for the themes and purposes of National Hospital Week, allowing Congress to officially endorse efforts to recognize hospitals during the designated week."
