summary: "# Summary: American Stroke Month Recognition (May 2026)  This resolution expresses Congressional support for designating May 2026 as \"American Stroke Month.\" The measure is intended to raise public awareness about stroke as a health issue and encourage Americans to learn about stroke warning signs, personal risk factors, and prevention strategies. The resolution does not create new programs, funding, or legal requirements; rather, it is a statement of support for a designated awareness period.  The resolution affects the general public by promoting education and awareness efforts related to stroke prevention, response, and recovery. It may encourage health organizations, medical professionals, medical professionals, and community groups to conduct outreach during the designated month. Individuals are encouraged to take personal action, such as learning warning signs of stroke (commonly remembered as F.A.S.T.: Face drooping, Arm weakness, Speech difficulty, and Time to call 911) and identifying their own risk factors.  Key elements include promotion of stroke awareness, education about prevention measures, and encouragement of community-level action to improve stroke response and recovery outcomes. As a resolution rather than legislation, this measure serves a symbolic purpose without direct fiscal impact or regulatory changes."
