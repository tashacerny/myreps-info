summary: "# Summary of National Gun Violence Awareness Day and Month Resolution  This resolution expresses Congressional support for designating June 6, 2025, as \"National Gun Violence Awareness Day\" and the entire month of June 2025 as \"National Gun Violence Awareness Month.\" The measure is a symbolic statement that does not create new laws or allocate funding; it is intended to draw national attention to the issue of gun violence and encourage public awareness and discussion during the designated period.  The resolution affects the general public by promoting awareness of gun violence as a national issue. It encourages individuals, organizations, and government agencies to participate in recognizing and discussing the topic during June 2025. The measure does not impose requirements or restrictions on any specific groups, but rather serves as an official expression of Congressional support for the awareness campaign.  Key aspects of the resolution include the establishment of the specific dates for recognition and the implicit invitation for Americans to use this designated time for education, commemoration, and dialogue related to gun violence. As a resolution rather than legislation, this measure does not change existing gun laws, funding, or policy—it functions as a statement of Congressional sentiment about the importance of acknowledging and addressing gun violence in America."
