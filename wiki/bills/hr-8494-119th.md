summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill would prohibit the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) from entering into, modifying, extending, or renewing contracts or intergovernmental service agreements related to establishing or operating new immigration detention facilities. The restrictions would apply to several types of detention structures, including warehouses, modular facilities, soft-sided structures, tent systems, and processing centers.  The bill would affect DHS operations and any contractors or government agencies that currently work with DHS on immigration detention. The restrictions would apply to new agreements and modifications to existing ones, though the bill does not explicitly address what happens to detention facilities already in operation under existing contracts.  The practical effect of the bill would be to freeze the development or expansion of immigration detention infrastructure in new or alternative facility types, potentially limiting DHS's flexibility in managing immigration detention capacity."
