summary: "# Summary: Muslim Brotherhood Terrorist Designation Act of 2025  **What the Bill Proposes**  This bill would direct the U.S. government to designate the Muslim Brotherhood as a foreign terrorist organization (FTO). Currently, the Muslim Brotherhood is not officially designated as an FTO by the U.S. State Department, though some of its affiliated organizations and splinter groups carry that designation. If passed, this bill would require the executive branch to formally classify the entire organization under existing terrorist designation laws.  **Who It Affects**  The designation would have legal consequences for individuals and entities associated with the Muslim Brotherhood, including potential restrictions on fundraising, financial transactions, travel, and immigration status in the United States. It could affect Muslim Brotherhood members, supporters, and affiliated charities or organizations. The designation could also influence U.S. diplomatic and foreign policy relationships with countries where the Muslim Brotherhood operates or holds political influence.  **Key Provisions**  While the full text is unavailable, such bills typically require or direct the State Department to make a formal FTO designation through established legal procedures. The designation would activate existing criminal and civil penalties under U.S. law for anyone providing material support to the designated organization."
