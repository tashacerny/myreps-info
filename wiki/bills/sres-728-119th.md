summary: "# Summary of Senate Resolution on Detainees in China  This Senate resolution expresses the chamber's position that the President should prioritize securing the release of five individuals detained by the People's Republic of China. The named detainees are: Pastor Jin Mingri, Pastor Gao Quanfu and his wife Pang Yu, Jimmy Lai, Dr. Gulshan Abbas, and Ekpar Asat. The resolution calls for the President to raise these cases during future diplomatic engagements with Chinese President Xi Jinping.  The resolution is a statement of Senate sentiment rather than binding legislation. It does not create new law or allocate funding, but instead formally expresses Congress's view on a foreign policy matter. The affected parties include the named detainees, their families, and potentially broader U.S.-China diplomatic relations. The resolution addresses cases involving individuals from various backgrounds—religious leaders, activists, and others—whose detention has been a subject of international concern."
