summary: "# Summary of Mental Health Awareness in Agriculture Day Resolution  This resolution designates May 29, 2025, as \"Mental Health Awareness in Agriculture Day.\" The measure is a symbolic action expressing support for a designated day intended to focus public and policy attention on mental health issues within the agricultural sector and among agricultural workers.  The resolution affects agricultural workers, farmers, agricultural organizations, and the broader public by establishing a day dedicated to raising awareness about mental health challenges specific to the agriculture industry. It aims to reduce the stigma associated with mental illness within agricultural communities, which may face unique stressors including economic pressures, isolation, and weather-related uncertainties.  The key provision is the official designation of the date itself as a day for awareness and advocacy efforts. Resolutions of this type typically encourage organizations, agencies, and individuals to recognize and promote the designated cause but do not establish new programs, funding, or legal requirements. The resolution serves as a statement of legislative support for mental health awareness in agriculture."
