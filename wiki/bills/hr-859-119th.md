The bill would primarily affect manufacturers, retailers, and sellers of smart devices, as well as consumers purchasing these products. Small manufacturers might face compliance costs, though the requirements would presumably be designed to provide meaningful but practical disclosures.  Key provisions would likely include requirements for manufacturers to disclose data collection practices, data sharing practices, security features, and user rights related to their smart devices.
