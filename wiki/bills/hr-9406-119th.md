summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill proposes to amend federal law governing the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) by removing a numerical limit on waivers the VA Secretary can issue to bypass certain pay restrictions for critical healthcare workers. Currently, law caps how many times the VA Secretary can waive standard pay limitations for these employees. The bill would eliminate this cap entirely, allowing the Secretary to issue an unlimited number of such waivers.  The measure affects VA healthcare personnel in positions deemed critical to the department's operations. It applies to employees whose compensation would otherwise be restricted by existing federal pay caps or limitations. The bill would give the VA Secretary greater flexibility to offer competitive compensation packages to recruit and retain healthcare workers in positions the department identifies as essential.  **Key provision:** Removal of the numerical cap on pay waivers for critical VA healthcare personnel, giving the VA Secretary authority to approve waivers without a preset limit on how many may be issued."
