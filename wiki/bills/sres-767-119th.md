summary: "# Bill Summary  This resolution celebrates the 2026 FIFA World Cup and acknowledges its historic significance as the first World Cup tournament to be jointly hosted by three nations: the United States, Canada, and Mexico. The resolution expresses welcome to the international community and recognizes the event as a major sporting occasion coming to North America.  As a resolution (rather than legislation), this measure does not create new laws or direct government spending. It is a symbolic statement of recognition and support. The resolution affects the three host nations and their residents by formally acknowledging the tournament's importance, and it serves to welcome international participants and visitors. Key elements include recognition of the unprecedented three-nation hosting arrangement and the significance of bringing the World Cup to North America."
