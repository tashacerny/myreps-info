summary: "# Summary of National Speech and Debate Education Day Resolution  This resolution designates March 7, 2025, as \"National Speech and Debate Education Day.\" The measure is a ceremonial designation intended to recognize and promote speech and debate education programs in the United States. The resolution does not create new laws, funding, or regulatory requirements; rather, it sets aside a specific date for national recognition of these educational activities.  The designation primarily affects speech and debate educators, students, and organizations that support these programs. Schools, debate leagues, and educational institutions may use the designated day to highlight the value of speech and debate education to their communities. The resolution acknowledges the role these programs play in developing communication skills, critical thinking, and public speaking abilities among students.  Key aspects of the measure include establishing the specific date of March 7, 2025, for the observance and the general purpose of promoting awareness and appreciation for speech and debate education. As a resolution rather than legislation, it carries symbolic value and does not obligate spending or regulatory action."
