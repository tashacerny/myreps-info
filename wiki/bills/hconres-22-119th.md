summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill authorizes the use of Emancipation Hall, located in the Capitol Visitor Center, for a specific ceremonial event. The ceremony will present Congressional Gold Medals that were previously awarded under the \"Six Triple Eight\" Congressional Gold Medal Act of 2021. The Six Triple Eight refers to the 6888th Central Postal Directory Battalion, an all-Black female Army unit that served during World War II.  The bill permits this particular group to hold their medal presentation ceremony in this congressional space, which is a designated venue for significant national events and commemorations. By authorizing this use, Congress designates Emancipation Hall as the location where these recipients can be publicly honored for their military service and historical significance."
