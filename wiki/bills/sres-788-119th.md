summary: "# Bill Summary  This resolution commemorates the fifth anniversary of the Champlain Towers South condominium collapse in Surfside, Florida, which occurred on June 24, 2021. The collapse resulted in 98 deaths and was one of the deadliest structural failures in U.S. history. As a commemorative resolution, this measure expresses the sentiment of the legislative body regarding this tragedy and its impact on the affected community.  The resolution directly affects residents of Surfside and the broader Miami-Dade County area, as well as the families of those who died or were injured in the collapse. It also acknowledges first responders and recovery workers who participated in the rescue and recovery efforts. While commemorative resolutions do not create new laws or programs, they serve to formally recognize significant events and may direct attention to related policy matters such as building safety standards or disaster preparedness."
