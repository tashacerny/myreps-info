Indirectly, it could affect families and communities where such individuals reside.  **Key Provisions** While specific details would depend on the bill's full text, legislation of this type typically includes mechanisms to: identify non-citizens with gang affiliations; expedite removal proceedings; provide law enforcement with tools to document gang membership; and potentially remove certain legal protections or appeal options for those designated as gang members.
