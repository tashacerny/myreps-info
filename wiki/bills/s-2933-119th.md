summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill proposes to rename a playground located within New River Gorge National Park and Preserve in West Virginia. The new name would be \"Hearts of Gold Playground: In Honor of West Virginia Children and Families Impacted by Childhood Cancer.\" The redesignation is intended as a tribute to children and families in the state who have been affected by childhood cancer.  The bill affects the National Park Service, which manages the park, and visitors to the New River Gorge National Park and Preserve. The change is administrative in nature and involves updating signage and official park designations to reflect the playground's new commemorative name.  This type of legislation is typically noncontroversial and does not carry fiscal implications. It falls under the category of commemorative bills that recognize specific causes or populations through the renaming of public facilities."
