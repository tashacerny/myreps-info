summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill proposes to amend the Veterans Community Care Program (VCCP) administered by the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) to broaden eligibility for veterans seeking mental health and substance-use disorder services. The VCCP currently allows the VA to refer veterans to non-VA providers in their communities when VA facilities cannot furnish needed services in a timely manner or when veterans live in areas without adequate VA resources. This bill would expand that program to explicitly include additional veterans seeking mental health treatment or substance-abuse services.  The bill would affect veterans who currently may not qualify for community care referrals under existing VCCP criteria but who need mental health or substance-use services. By expanding the program, more veterans would be able to access treatment through private providers and community organizations rather than waiting for VA appointments or traveling to VA facilities. This could increase the range of treatment options available to veterans in their local areas.  Key provisions would likely include defining which additional categories of veterans gain access to the program for these services and establishing any conditions or requirements for their participation. The bill may also address administrative processes for referrals and payment mechanisms for community providers treating veterans under the expanded program."
