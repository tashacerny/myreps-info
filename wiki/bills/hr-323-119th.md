Designating a federal building as a memorial does not change its operations or functions. The post office will continue to serve its regular purpose of providing postal services to the Avon community. The primary effect of the bill is ceremonial—establishing an official name that honors the individual being memorialized. This type of legislation typically requires minimal federal resources and affects only the official naming and signage of the facility."
