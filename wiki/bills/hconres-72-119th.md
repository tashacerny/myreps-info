summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill authorizes the use of Emancipation Hall, located in the Capitol Visitor Center, for a ceremony commemorating the days of remembrance of victims of the Holocaust. The legislation permits a specific event or series of events to take place in this Capitol facility as part of Holocaust remembrance activities.  The bill primarily affects the scheduling and use of Capitol space. It allows Holocaust commemoration organizers to hold their ceremony in Emancipation Hall rather than requiring them to seek alternative venues. The authorization would need to comply with standard Capitol facility use policies and security procedures.  This type of legislation is procedural in nature, granting permission for a particular commemorative use of a public Capitol facility. No regulatory changes, funding allocations, or programmatic requirements are indicated by the bill's title."
