The designation would primarily affect the postal facility itself and its operations in St. Louis. The bill does not appear to involve appropriations, operational changes, or alterations to postal services. This type of legislation is a common practice in Congress, where post offices and other federal facilities are named to honor individuals. The facility would continue its standard postal operations under the new official designation."
