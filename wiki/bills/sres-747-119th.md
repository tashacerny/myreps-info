summary: "# Summary of Renewable Fuels Month Resolution  This resolution expresses congressional support for designating May 2026 as \"Renewable Fuels Month.\" The designation is intended to recognize the contributions of renewable fuels—such as ethanol and biodiesel—to several national interests, including reducing consumer fuel costs, decreasing dependence on foreign energy sources, supporting rural economies, and lowering carbon emissions.  The resolution is primarily symbolic and does not create new law or establish binding requirements. It affects no specific groups through regulatory or financial changes. Rather, it serves as a statement of congressional recognition intended to draw public attention to renewable fuels and their role in energy production and consumption during the designated month.  Key aspects of the resolution include its focus on four stated benefits: economic (lower prices and rural support), national security (reducing reliance on foreign sources), and environmental (reducing carbon impacts). As a resolution, this measure does not require presidential signature to pass and carries no enforcement mechanism—it functions as an expression of the views of Congress on the subject matter."
