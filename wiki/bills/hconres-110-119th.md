This bill uses that authority to mandate a withdrawal from Iran-related hostilities.  **Key Effects:** The bill would apply to the President and the U.S. military. If enacted, it would require the removal of armed forces engaged in or preparing for combat operations involving Iran, absent separate congressional authorization or a formal declaration of war.  **Scope:** Without access to the full text, the specific scope cannot be detailed, but such bills typically cover direct combat operations while potentially creating exceptions for defensive actions, intelligence operations, or protection of U.S. personnel.
