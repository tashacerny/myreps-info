summary: "# Bill Summary: Scipio A. Jones House Special Resource Study  **Purpose and Scope** This bill directs the Secretary of the Interior to conduct a special resource study of the Scipio A. Jones House located in Little Rock, Arkansas. A special resource study is a formal evaluation used to determine whether a site meets criteria for inclusion in the National Park System or deserves other forms of federal protection and management.  **What the Study Would Examine** The study would assess the historical, cultural, and natural significance of the Scipio A. Jones House and evaluate options for its preservation and public access. This type of study typically examines the property's historical importance, its condition, the feasibility of various management approaches, and estimated costs for different preservation scenarios.  **Who Is Affected** The bill primarily affects the Department of the Interior and potentially local stakeholders in Little Rock. If the study determines the house warrants National Park System designation or similar protection, it could lead to federal involvement in managing the site. The study itself does not authorize land acquisition or establish a protected area; rather, it provides information to Congress for future decision-making about the site's management and preservation."
