summary: "# Summary: National Pollinator Week Designation  This bill designates June 15 through June 22, 2025, as \"National Pollinator Week\" in the United States. The designation is intended to recognize the ecological and agricultural importance of pollinators—such as bees, butterflies, and other insects that facilitate plant reproduction by transferring pollen between flowers.  The bill does not create new laws, funding, or regulatory requirements. Instead, it is a commemorative measure that formally acknowledges a specific week for public awareness and recognition. The designation may be used to encourage educational activities, events, and outreach by government agencies, nonprofits, agricultural organizations, and others interested in pollinator conservation and protection.  The measure affects no specific individuals or organizations directly, though it may benefit pollinator advocacy efforts by providing an official focal point for awareness campaigns during that week."
