summary: "# Summary of National Salvation Army Week Resolution  This bill proposes to officially designate May 11–17, 2026, as \"National Salvation Army Week.\" The resolution aims to recognize and celebrate the Salvation Army, a charitable and religious organization that operates community assistance programs nationwide. The designation would be a formal congressional acknowledgment of the organization's work and mission.  The bill affects the Salvation Army organization and its supporters, as well as the general public through official recognition. The key provision is simply the establishment of this one-week observance period in 2026. No funding, regulatory changes, or programmatic requirements are involved—the resolution serves as a symbolic gesture of recognition rather than creating any new government actions or obligations."
