summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill proposes to rename the Christiansted Bandstand, located at the Christiansted National Historic Site in St. Croix, Virgin Islands, to the \"Peter G. Thurland, Sr., Bandstand.\" The change is a commemorative designation that would honor Peter G. Thurland, Sr., presumably for contributions related to the historic site or the community.  The bill affects the National Park Service, which manages the Christiansted National Historic Site, and would apply to all official references, signage, and materials related to the bandstand. The practical impact would be limited to updating the official name used by park administration and in public materials. No new programs, funding, or operational changes are proposed."
