summary: "# Summary of Endangered Species Day Bill  This bill proposes to designate May 16, 2025, as \"Endangered Species Day\" in the United States. The designation is a symbolic recognition day intended to raise public awareness about endangered and threatened species and the importance of conservation efforts.  The bill primarily affects public messaging and education. It does not create new laws, funding, or regulatory requirements. Instead, it authorizes the federal government to officially recognize the date, which may be marked by government agencies, educational institutions, and organizations with events, communications, and activities focused on endangered species issues.  This type of designation bill is a common legislative practice used to highlight particular causes or observances. It allows Congress to formally recognize topics it considers significant for public attention and understanding."
