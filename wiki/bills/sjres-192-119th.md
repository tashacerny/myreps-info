summary: "# Bill Summary  This joint resolution seeks to disapprove a rule issued by the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) under the Congressional Review Act (CRA). The rule being challenged pertains to the \"Wasteful and Inappropriate Services Reduction (WISeR) Model,\" which implements prior authorization requirements for select services under the Medicare program. Prior authorization is a process requiring healthcare providers to obtain approval from Medicare before delivering certain services to beneficiaries.  The resolution would affect Medicare beneficiaries, healthcare providers who treat Medicare patients, and the CMS implementation of this payment model. If passed, it would nullify the WISeR Model rule. Under the Congressional Review Act, Congress can disapprove federal agency rules through this expedited process, and if a rule is disapproved, the agency cannot subsequently issue a substantially similar rule without new legislative authority.  The key provision is the disapproval of the WISeR Model's prior authorization requirements for select Medicare services. The resolution does not propose alternative policies but simply seeks to prevent the rule from taking effect or to reverse it if already implemented."
