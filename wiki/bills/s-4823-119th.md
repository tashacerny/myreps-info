summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill proposes to amend the Export-Import Bank Act of 1945 to expand the scope of the Program on China and Transformational Exports. Specifically, it would add quantum information science and technology to the list of technology areas covered by this program. The Export-Import Bank (EXIM Bank) is a federal agency that provides financing, insurance, and other support to help U.S. companies export goods and services.  The bill would affect EXIM Bank's operations and potentially U.S. companies involved in quantum technology development and export. By including quantum information science and technology in the program, EXIM Bank would be authorized to provide financing and export support for U.S. quantum technology companies, similar to how it currently supports exports in other transformational technology sectors. This expansion reflects congressional interest in advancing U.S. competitiveness in quantum technologies, which have applications in computing, communications, and sensing."
