summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill proposes to amend the Small Business Act by adding new requirements related to apprenticeship programs at small business development centers (SBDCs). Small business development centers are federally funded organizations that provide counseling, training, and resources to help small businesses start, grow, and succeed. The bill would require these centers to incorporate apprenticeship program assistance into their services, meaning they would help small business owners understand and potentially establish apprenticeship programs.  The bill would directly affect small business development centers and the small business owners they serve. It would also impact potential apprentices seeking training opportunities. By requiring SBDCs to provide guidance on apprenticeships, the bill aims to connect small businesses with a workforce development tool that combines on-the-job training with classroom instruction.  The key provision is the mandate that SBDCs include apprenticeship program assistance as part of their service offerings. This would likely involve training SBDC counselors on apprenticeship programs and ensuring they can advise small business clients on how to develop, fund, and manage such programs. The bill's phrase \"and for other purposes\" suggests it may contain additional provisions not evident from the title alone."
