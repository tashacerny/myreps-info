summary: "# Bill Summary  This resolution expresses Congressional support for designating May 2026 as \"Progressive Supranuclear Palsy and Corticobasal Degeneration Awareness Month.\" The resolution does not create any new laws or funding; instead, it is a symbolic measure intended to recognize and raise public awareness of two rare neurological disorders during the specified month.  Progressive Supranuclear Palsy (PSP) and Corticobasal Degeneration (CBD) are progressive brain disorders that affect movement, balance, and cognitive function. The resolution would acknowledge these conditions and encourage public and private organizations to promote awareness and education about them during May 2026. This measure affects advocacy groups, medical organizations, and the public by providing a designated timeframe for awareness activities, though it carries no direct budgetary or regulatory consequences."
