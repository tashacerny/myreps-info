summary: "# Bill Summary  This resolution expresses the House of Representatives' position that President Donald Trump, Special Envoy Steven Witkoff, and all federal officials must comply with the Foreign Emoluments Clause of the U.S. Constitution. The Foreign Emoluments Clause prohibits federal officials from accepting payments or benefits from foreign governments without congressional approval. The resolution calls for immediate surrender of any such payments to the Department of the Treasury and the divestment of business interests connected to foreign governments.  The resolution would affect the President, his designated special envoy, and all federal officials. It specifically addresses concerns about potential financial ties to the United Arab Emirates and other foreign states. As a resolution expressing the sense of the House, this measure communicates the chamber's collective position on constitutional compliance rather than imposing legally binding requirements; enforcement of the Foreign Emoluments Clause itself depends on the constitutional text and interpretation by courts or Congress."
