summary: "# Summary of \"National Wildfire Preparedness Month\" Resolution  This resolution designates May 2026 as \"National Wildfire Preparedness Month.\" The measure is a symbolic designation that recognizes and promotes awareness of wildfire preparedness across the United States during that month. Resolutions of this type do not create new programs or funding, but instead encourage public attention and education around a specific topic.  The resolution primarily affects public messaging and awareness efforts. It encourages federal agencies, state and local governments, nonprofit organizations, and private citizens to highlight wildfire prevention, preparedness planning, and response readiness during May 2026. This may include educational campaigns, community preparedness events, and coordination among fire management agencies. The designation aims to increase public understanding of wildfire risks and the importance of preparation before wildfire season peaks in many regions."
