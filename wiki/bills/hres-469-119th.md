summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill would modify House rules to permit official photographs of the House of Representatives to be taken during active legislative sessions on dates chosen by the Speaker of the House. Currently, House rules restrict when official photography can occur during sessions. The bill would create an exception to these restrictions for designated photography dates approved by the Speaker.  The change would affect House operations and media access during floor proceedings. It could impact House members, official photographers, and news organizations that cover congressional activities. The key provision is the authorization for the Speaker to designate specific dates when official photographs may be taken while the House conducts legislative business, rather than limiting photography to times when the chamber is not in session."
