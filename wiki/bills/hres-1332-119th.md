summary: "# Summary of Blue Star Mother's Day Bill  This bill proposes to establish February 1st as \"Blue Star Mother's Day,\" a day of national recognition for mothers of active-duty military service members. The designation would honor and acknowledge the contributions and sacrifices of these mothers while their children serve in the U.S. Armed Forces.  The bill primarily affects Blue Star mothers—a recognized group of women whose adult children are currently serving on active duty in the military. The Blue Star designation has historical roots, originating during World War II when families displayed blue stars in their windows to indicate they had a family member in active service. The bill would not create any new federal programs or appropriations; rather, it establishes a symbolic day for national observance and recognition.  The key provision is the official designation of February 1st as \"Blue Star Mother's Day\" at the federal level. This would allow for official recognition through proclamations and observances, similar to other designated national days. The measure is intended to raise awareness of the experiences of military families and provide a formal acknowledgment of the sacrifices made by mothers throughout their children's military service."
