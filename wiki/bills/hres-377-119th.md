summary: "# Summary of House Rule  This resolution is a procedural measure that sets the terms for floor consideration of two separate bills in the House of Representatives.  **H.R. 276** proposes to officially rename the Gulf of Mexico to the \"Gulf of America\" in federal law and usage. This would affect how the federal government refers to the body of water in official documents, maps, and communications.  **H.R. 881** would restrict Department of Homeland Security (DHS) funding to institutions of higher education that maintain relationships with Confucius Institutes—Chinese government-sponsored cultural and educational organizations operating on U.S. college campuses. The bill targets colleges and universities that have such partnerships, potentially affecting their eligibility for certain federal funding.  As a rule resolution, this measure does not enact policy itself but rather establishes the parliamentary procedures—such as debate time limits and amendment processes—under which the House will consider these two bills."
