summary: "# Summary of National Day of Reason Bill  This is a commemorative resolution that expresses support for designating May 4, 2025, as a \"National Day of Reason.\" The bill does not create new laws or establish binding obligations; rather, it is a statement of congressional support for recognizing a specific observance date. The measure acknowledges reason—defined in the resolution's purpose as rational thought and evidence-based decision-making—as important to human progress and well-being.  The bill affects no specific regulated parties or individuals directly, as commemorative resolutions are non-binding statements of congressional sentiment. The designation is intended as a cultural or observance recognition rather than a legal requirement. Any observance of the date would be voluntary on the part of individuals, organizations, or communities.  The key provision is the designation itself: setting aside May 4, 2025, as a national day to recognize and reflect on the role of reason, critical thinking, and scientific inquiry in addressing societal challenges and improving human conditions. The resolution serves a primarily symbolic purpose of encouraging public awareness and discussion around these themes."
