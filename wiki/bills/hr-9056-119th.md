summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill would direct the Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) to permit certain grant recipients to use Flood Mitigation Assistance Grant funds—and potentially other grant programs—to pay premiums for community-based parametric flood insurance policies. Parametric insurance pays out a predetermined amount based on the occurrence and severity of a specific event (in this case, flooding), rather than waiting for damage assessment and claims processing. The bill aims to make this insurance option available as an eligible use of federal mitigation grant funds.  The bill would affect communities and organizations that currently receive FEMA flood mitigation grants, as well as FEMA's administration of these programs. It expands what grant recipients can use their federal funds for, potentially offering them an alternative risk management tool alongside traditional flood mitigation projects like infrastructure improvements or buyout programs.  The key provision is the authorization for grant recipients to redirect portions of their mitigation assistance to purchase parametric flood insurance policies at the community level. The phrase \"and for other purposes\" suggests the bill may contain additional provisions not evident from the title alone, though the primary focus appears to be enabling this insurance mechanism as a mitigation strategy."
