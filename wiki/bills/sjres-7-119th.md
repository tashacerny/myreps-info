summary: "# Bill Summary  This joint resolution seeks to disapprove a Federal Communications Commission (FCC) rule titled \"Addressing the Homework Gap Through the E-Rate Program.\" Under the Congressional Review Act (a federal law that allows Congress to overturn agency regulations), this resolution would block the FCC rule from taking effect or remaining in effect if it has already been implemented.  The E-Rate Program provides federal funding to schools and libraries to help them obtain affordable internet access and telecommunications services. The FCC rule in question addresses the \"homework gap\"—the problem of students lacking adequate internet access at home to complete schoolwork. The specific provisions of the rule are not detailed in the title, but it presumably modifies or expands how E-Rate funds can be used to help close this access divide.  If passed, this resolution would affect the FCC's ability to implement or enforce this particular rule. It would also impact schools, libraries, and potentially students who might otherwise benefit from any expanded E-Rate funding or program changes the rule would have authorized. Congress would need to pass the resolution, and the President would need to sign it, for the disapproval to take effect."
