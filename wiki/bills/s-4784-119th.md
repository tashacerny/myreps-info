It covers military operations, weapons systems, personnel, facilities, and related defense activities. The bill also sets authorized personnel strength levels—the number of active duty, reserve, and National Guard service members—for each military branch. These authorizations determine the funding available for defense activities, though separate appropriations bills must still approve the actual spending.  The bill affects military personnel, defense contractors, civilian Department of Defense employees, and the Department of Energy's national security operations.
