summary: "# Bill Summary: Literacy Outcomes and Science of Reading  This bill aims to improve reading instruction and literacy outcomes for students by amending existing federal literacy grant programs and increasing accountability measures. The legislation focuses on aligning literacy instruction with scientific research on how students learn to read—commonly referred to as the \"science of reading.\" It proposes to strengthen the comprehensive literacy State development grant program, which provides federal funding to states for literacy initiatives.  The bill affects states, school districts, teachers, and students across the country. It establishes requirements for federal accountability in how literacy programs are implemented and evaluated. Key provisions include directing investment toward research on effective reading instruction methods, supporting teacher preparation and professional development in evidence-based literacy practices, and requiring that federal funding support instruction grounded in scientific evidence about reading development.  Overall, the bill emphasizes using data-driven, research-backed approaches to literacy instruction while increasing federal oversight and support for these efforts at the state and local level."
