summary: "# Summary of H. Res. [Number]  This is a procedural resolution that establishes the terms for considering four separate bills in the House of Representatives. The resolution itself does not create new policy; rather, it sets the rules and timeline for debating and voting on each of the four bills listed. This type of measure is a \"rule\" that the House uses to organize its legislative schedule and define how each bill will be handled on the chamber floor.  The four bills covered by this resolution address distinct policy areas: H.R. 139 would make daylight savings time permanent year-round; H.R. 8595 contains appropriations for national security, the Department of State, and related agencies for fiscal year 2027; H.R. 9237 would modify veterans' benefits and improve Department of Veterans Affairs operations; and H.R. 1181 would restrict payment card networks from using merchant category codes that single out firearms retailers. The resolution allows the House to consider all four bills under a single procedural framework.  The resolution affects the legislative process for these bills but does not directly impact the public until and unless the underlying bills are passed and enacted into law."
