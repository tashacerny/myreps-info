summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill proposes to extend the Quality Payment Program-Small Practice, Underserved, and Rural Support (QPPSMURS) program under Medicare (Title XVIII of the Social Security Act). The QPPSMURS program is designed to provide support and alternative pathways for healthcare providers who participate in Medicare's Quality Payment Program (QPP), particularly those in smaller practices, underserved areas, or rural regions.  The bill primarily affects healthcare providers—including physicians, advanced practice clinicians, and other Medicare participants—who operate in small practices, underserved communities, or rural areas. These providers often face greater administrative and financial burdens when complying with Medicare's performance and payment requirements compared to larger healthcare organizations.  Key provisions would extend the program's authorization, allowing eligible providers to continue accessing streamlined reporting requirements, exemptions from certain penalties, and other support mechanisms. By extending this program, the bill aims to maintain assistance for providers serving populations and areas where healthcare access challenges are greater, and where the infrastructure for complex Medicare compliance may be more limited."
