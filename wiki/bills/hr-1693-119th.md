summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill proposes to rename the National Historic Trails Interpretive Center in Casper, Wyoming. The facility would be redesignated from its current name to the \"Barbara L. Cubin National Historic Trails Interpretive Center.\" The bill affects only the official name of this federally-affiliated visitor center, with no changes to its operations, funding, or functions.  The center, which interprets and educates visitors about historic trails in the region, would honor Barbara L. Cubin through this redesignation. The change would apply to all official references to the facility, signage, and government documents. No significant operational or budgetary impacts would result from the name change."
