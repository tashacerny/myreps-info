summary: "# Bill Summary: Semiquincentennial Congressional Time Capsule Dedication  This bill authorizes the use of Emancipation Hall for a ceremonial event on June 24, 2026, to dedicate a Congressional Time Capsule commemorating the 250th anniversary of the United States (the \"Semiquincentennial\"). The bill permits a one-time use of this Capitol building space for this specific dedication ceremony.  The bill affects Congress and those involved in organizing the Semiquincentennial commemoration events. Emancipation Hall, located in the Capitol building, would be made available for the scheduled ceremony. The time capsule is intended to mark the nation's 250th anniversary, which occurs in 2026. No other major operational or policy changes are indicated based on the bill's title and scope."
