summary: "# Summary: Wildfire Preparedness Week Recognition (May 4–10, 2025)  This resolution expresses Congressional support for designating May 4–10, 2025, as \"Wildfire Preparedness Week,\" a national observance focused on public education about fire safety and wildfire preparedness. The bill does not create new law or appropriations; rather, it formally recognizes and endorses an existing awareness initiative.  The resolution supports the educational goals of Wildfire Preparedness Week, which aims to increase public knowledge about fire prevention, emergency preparedness, and protective measures related to wildfires. The observance affects the general public and communities nationwide, particularly those in wildfire-prone regions, by promoting awareness of fire safety practices and emergency response measures.  As a resolution expressing support, this measure carries no regulatory requirements or spending authority. Its primary effect is to lend Congressional recognition to the awareness week, potentially helping to amplify public outreach efforts by federal agencies, state and local governments, nonprofits, and private organizations involved in wildfire safety and preparedness."
