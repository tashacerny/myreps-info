summary: "# Bill Summary: DIPG Awareness Day Designation  This bill expresses congressional support for designating May 17, 2025, as \"DIPG Awareness Day.\" DIPG (diffuse intrinsic pontine glioma) is a rare and aggressive form of brain cancer that primarily affects children. The designation is intended to raise public awareness about DIPG and encourage increased research efforts toward finding treatments and cures for this condition and pediatric cancers more broadly.  The bill does not establish mandatory requirements or allocate funding. Rather, it is a symbolic measure expressing support for a national awareness day. The measure affects patients with DIPG and their families, as well as researchers, advocacy organizations, and others working on pediatric cancer initiatives who may use the designated day to highlight the disease and related research needs."
