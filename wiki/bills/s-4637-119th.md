summary: "# Bill Summary: J-1 Nonimmigrant Physician Flexibility  **Purpose and Scope**  This bill addresses employment opportunities for foreign physicians in the United States who hold J-1 visas (exchange visitor visas). It proposes two main changes: increasing the number of \"flex waivers\" available to each state by 5, and creating a new online portal to help physicians find employment across state lines.  **Key Provisions**  The flex waiver increase allows states to authorize an additional 5 foreign physicians to work outside their initially assigned locations or specialties, providing greater flexibility in physician placement. The secondary physician match portal would serve as a centralized system enabling physicians unable to secure employment in their preferred state to identify and apply for positions in other states. This addresses a practical barrier where qualified physicians may face limited job opportunities in particular geographic areas or medical specialties.  **Who It Affects**  The bill primarily affects foreign-trained physicians on J-1 visas, as well as state health workforce agencies that administer physician placement programs. It may indirectly benefit medical facilities and communities seeking to address physician shortages by expanding the pool of available physicians who can relocate to underserved areas."
