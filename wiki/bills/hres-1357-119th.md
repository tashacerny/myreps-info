summary: "# Summary  This bill formally acknowledges and apologizes to lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender (LGBT) individuals who served or serve in the U.S. military, Foreign Service, and federal civil service for past mistreatment and discrimination they experienced. The bill recognizes historical policies and practices that affected LGBT service members and federal employees, including dismissals, security clearance denials, and other discriminatory treatment.  The bill commits the federal government to pursuing equal rights, protections, and respect for all LGBT servicemembers and federal civil servants going forward. It affects current and former members of the military, Foreign Service personnel, and federal employees. The measure is primarily symbolic and procedural in nature—it establishes a formal congressional statement of acknowledgment and apology rather than creating new statutory requirements or funding allocations."
