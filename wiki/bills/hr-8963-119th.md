In this case, the medal recognizes Sekula's heroic service and life.  The bill would affect the historical record by officially honoring Sekula's military service through this legislative action. The medal itself would be a symbolic recognition, and the bill may also include provisions for the design and minting of the medal, as well as arrangements for its display or presentation to his family or a designated institution. Congressional Gold Medal bills typically direct the Secretary of the Treasury to strike the medal and may authorize its public exhibition.  As a posthumous award, this bill honors an individual no longer living.
