summary: "# Summary of National Learn to Swim Week Designation Bill  This bill proposes to establish an official \"National Learn to Swim Week\" to be observed annually beginning on the 4th Sunday in June. The designation would create a named week intended to promote swimming education and water safety awareness across the United States.  The bill affects swimming organizations, schools, community centers, and public health advocates who focus on water safety and swimming instruction. It does not appear to establish funding, mandates, or requirements; rather, it designates a specific week for recognition and promotion of swimming education initiatives. The timing in June aligns with the start of summer, when swimming activity and water-related recreation typically increase.  Key aspects of the proposal include the official designation of the specific timeframe (the 4th Sunday in June) and the implicit encouragement for organizations to use this week to raise awareness about the importance of swimming skills and water safety. Such designations typically serve to highlight public health or safety issues and coordinate nationwide awareness efforts, though implementation and participation would be voluntary."
