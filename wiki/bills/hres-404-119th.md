summary: "# Bill Summary: National Salvation Army Week Recognition  This bill proposes to officially recognize and celebrate \"National Salvation Army Week\" during May 12–18, 2025. The measure is a ceremonial designation that does not create new laws, regulations, or funding. It would direct Congress to acknowledge the Salvation Army organization during this specific week.  The bill affects the Salvation Army, a humanitarian and religious organization that provides social services including disaster relief, food assistance, housing support, and rehabilitation programs. The recognition would also apply broadly to the public, as it represents an official congressional acknowledgment of the organization's work during the designated week.  The key provision is simply the establishment of the May 12–18, 2025 timeframe as the official national observance period. Designations of this type are typically used to increase public awareness of organizations or causes and have no direct budgetary or regulatory impact."
