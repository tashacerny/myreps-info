summary: "# Bill Summary: Small Business Administration Disaster Loans for HOAs  This bill proposes amending the Small Business Act to create a new loan program within the Small Business Administration (SBA) that would provide supplemental disaster loans to home owner associations (HOAs). The program would allow HOAs to borrow funds to repair common areas—such as shared buildings, grounds, and infrastructure—that have been damaged by declared disasters. Additionally, the loans could be used to implement disaster mitigation measures designed to reduce future damage from similar events.  The bill would directly affect HOAs located in disaster-affected areas, as well as the homeowners who are members of those associations. Currently, HOAs may have limited access to federal disaster recovery resources available to individual homeowners and businesses. This program would expand SBA lending authority to include these community organizations as borrowers.  Key provisions would likely include establishing loan terms, interest rates, and eligibility requirements for participating HOAs, though the specific details are not available in the title alone. The program would address a gap in disaster recovery resources by enabling HOAs to restore and protect shared community assets that individual homeowners cannot repair themselves."
