The bill affects the postal facility itself and those who work there or interact with it. The primary practical impact is administrative—the building will be formally renamed to honor Congressman Gerald E. Connolly. This type of legislation is typically ceremonial in nature and does not change the facility's operations, services, or funding. The Postal Service and local officials in Fairfax, Virginia would be responsible for implementing and maintaining the new official designation."
