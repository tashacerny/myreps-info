summary: "# Bill Summary: National Native Plant Month Designation  This bill proposes to officially designate April 2025 as \"National Native Plant Month.\" The designation is a congressional recognition intended to raise awareness about native plants—plant species naturally occurring in a particular region—and their ecological and environmental importance.  The bill would affect organizations, agencies, and individuals involved in conservation, gardening, landscaping, and environmental education. It encourages recognition and promotion of native plants during the designated month through outreach, educational activities, and public awareness initiatives. The designation itself carries no direct regulatory requirements or funding obligations, but serves as a formal congressional endorsement that may support existing native plant promotion efforts by government agencies, nonprofits, and educational institutions.  Key provisions would include the official designation of April 2025 for this purpose. Such designations typically encourage federal agencies and organizations to highlight the topic through events, educational materials, and media outreach. Native plant initiatives can support environmental goals including habitat restoration, pollinator support, water conservation, and reduced pesticide use in landscaping and agriculture."
