summary: "# Summary: Women Veterans Appreciation Day Designation  This bill proposes to designate June 12, 2026, as \"Women Veterans Appreciation Day\" through a congressional resolution. The measure expresses support for formally recognizing this date to honor women who have served in the U.S. military. The bill affects all women veterans and raises public awareness about their contributions and service.  As a resolution rather than legislation, this bill does not create binding law or establish ongoing federal programs or funding. Instead, it serves as a symbolic statement of congressional support for acknowledging women veterans' military service on a designated date. The practical effects would include promoting recognition through statements, public acknowledgment, and potentially community-organized events on that date."
