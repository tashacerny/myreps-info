summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill would prohibit non-citizens from voting in elections held in the District of Columbia and would repeal the Local Resident Voting Rights Amendment Act of 2022. The bill affects DC residents and the voting eligibility rules that currently govern local elections in the district.  The key provision prohibits individuals who are not U.S. citizens from voting in DC elections. This would reverse or modify provisions of the 2022 Local Resident Voting Rights Amendment Act, which the bill seeks to repeal entirely. The practical effect would be to restrict voting in DC elections to U.S. citizens only, potentially removing voting rights for lawful permanent residents and other non-citizen residents who may have been granted voting eligibility under the 2022 act."
