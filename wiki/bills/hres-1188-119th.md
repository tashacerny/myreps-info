summary: "# Summary: Support for Open Water Lifeguards as First Responders  This resolution expresses congressional support for recognizing open water lifeguards—those who work at beaches, lakes, and other outdoor water venues—as first responders and emergency response providers. The measure acknowledges the critical role these professionals play in water safety and emergency situations.  The resolution affects open water lifeguards and the organizations that employ them, such as beach management agencies, state parks departments, and private beach facilities. It may also influence how these workers are classified, trained, and supported by government entities and emergency management systems.  Key provisions likely include formal recognition of lifeguards' first responder status, which could impact their access to training programs, benefits, professional development opportunities, and potentially their legal protections and compensation. Such recognition would align open water lifeguards with other recognized first responder categories like firefighters and emergency medical personnel."
