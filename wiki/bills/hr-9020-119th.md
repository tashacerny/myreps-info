summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill would prohibit the use of federal funds to support the United Nations Interim Force in Lebanon (UNIFIL) beginning October 1, 2027. UNIFIL is a UN peacekeeping mission established in 1978 that operates in southern Lebanon, primarily along the border with Israel. The bill would effectively end U.S. financial contributions to this peacekeeping operation on that specified date.  The bill affects the U.S. government's budget allocation for international peacekeeping operations and would impact the funding structure of UNIFIL, which relies partly on voluntary contributions from member states like the United States. It may also have diplomatic implications for U.S. relations with the UN, Lebanon, and other nations that support the mission. The bill sets a specific implementation date nearly three years in the future, potentially allowing time for transition planning.  Without access to the full text, the phrase \"and for other purposes\" suggests the bill may contain additional provisions beyond the UNIFIL funding prohibition, though those are not specified in the title alone."
