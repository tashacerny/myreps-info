summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill would invoke the War Powers Resolution to direct the President to withdraw U.S. Armed Forces from any ongoing or imminent hostilities with Iran. The War Powers Resolution (passed in 1973) allows Congress to require the President to end military operations within a specified timeframe without congressional authorization. This bill uses that mechanism to mandate removal of U.S. forces engaged in hostilities with Iran.  The bill would affect U.S. military personnel deployed in regions where they may be engaged in or at risk of hostilities involving Iran, as well as U.S. foreign policy and military operations in the Middle East more broadly. It could impact coordination with allies and regional partners who work alongside U.S. forces.  Key provisions would likely include: a requirement to cease hostilities and withdraw forces within a defined period; possible exceptions for force protection or other narrow military purposes (depending on specific language); and reporting requirements to Congress regarding implementation. The bill's scope and any carve-outs would depend on the full legislative text."
