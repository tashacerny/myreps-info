summary: "# Bill Summary  This resolution expresses congressional support for designating July 10, 2026, as Journeyman Lineworkers Recognition Day. The measure is a symbolic statement of recognition rather than legislation that creates binding requirements or enforcement mechanisms. It honors journeyman lineworkers—skilled tradespeople who install, maintain, and repair electrical power lines and related infrastructure.  The resolution primarily affects journeyman lineworkers and their industry by providing federal recognition of their profession and contributions to the nation's electrical infrastructure. It may also raise awareness among the general public about the role lineworkers play in maintaining reliable power systems. The designation does not create new programs, funding, or regulatory changes.  Key aspects of the measure include the establishment of an official recognition day and the implicit acknowledgment of journeyman lineworkers' essential role in the economy and daily functioning of electrical utilities across the country."
