summary: "# Bill Summary: Borderline Personality Disorder Awareness Month  This bill proposes to designate May 2026 as \"Borderline Personality Disorder Awareness Month\" at the federal level. The measure is a commemorative designation that would recognize and raise awareness of borderline personality disorder (BPD), a mental health condition characterized by unstable relationships, self-image, and emotions, along with impulsive behaviors.  The bill would primarily affect public awareness and education efforts rather than creating new programs or funding. It would encourage federal agencies, organizations, and the public to use the designated month to increase understanding of BPD, reduce stigma surrounding the condition, and provide information about available resources and treatments. The designation could benefit individuals living with BPD, their families and caregivers, mental health professionals, and the general public through increased education and visibility.  Key provisions of the bill include officially recognizing May 2026 for this purpose and potentially authorizing activities such as public information campaigns, educational events, and media outreach during that month. As a commemorative designation, the bill does not establish new federal spending, regulatory requirements, or direct services."
