summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill proposes to officially designate a specific United States Postal Service facility located at 300 Macedonia Lane in Knoxville, Tennessee, as the \"Reverend Harold Middlebrook Post Office Building.\" The designation is a naming honor for the facility rather than a change to its operations or services.  The bill directly affects the postal facility in Knoxville and would require the Postal Service to adopt and use the official name \"Reverend Harold Middlebrook Post Office Building\" for the location. This type of legislation allows Congress to recognize individuals by naming federal buildings after them. The bill would have no practical impact on postal operations, services, or budgets—it is solely a ceremonial naming designation. Anyone using the postal facility or referring to it would do so under the new official name."
