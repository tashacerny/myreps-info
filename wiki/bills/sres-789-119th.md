It expresses official recognition of the month as a time to acknowledge the lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and queer community and related observances.  The resolution affects no specific individuals or groups through regulatory requirements or legal obligations. Rather, it is a statement of official commemoration that may be used to encourage public awareness and observance of Pride Month during that period.  The key provision is simply the designation itself. Such resolutions are ceremonial measures that serve to mark occasions and express the legislature's position on cultural or historical recognition, without implementing substantive policy changes or financial impacts."
