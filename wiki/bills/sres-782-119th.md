summary: "# Summary of National Pollinator Week Resolution  This resolution designates the week of June 22–28, 2026, as \"National Pollinator Week\" in the United States. The resolution acknowledges the ecological and economic importance of pollinators—insects and animals that facilitate plant reproduction by transferring pollen—to both natural ecosystems and agricultural productivity across the country.  The designation serves as a formal recognition intended to increase public awareness of pollinators and their role in food production and environmental health. National Pollinator Week affects no individuals or organizations directly through regulatory requirements or funding changes, but rather functions as a symbolic measure to highlight the significance of pollinators and potentially encourage educational activities, outreach programs, and conservation efforts during that designated week."
