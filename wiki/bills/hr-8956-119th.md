summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill proposes to expand eligibility for special overtime compensation to U.S. Border Patrol agents who hold positions classified at grades above GS-12 (General Schedule pay grade 12). Currently, special overtime pay provisions for Border Patrol agents appear to be limited to those at or below a certain grade level. The bill would remove or adjust this grade-based restriction to include higher-ranking agents in the overtime pay program.  **Who It Affects:** The primary beneficiaries would be U.S. Border Patrol agents working in supervisory, management, or specialized roles classified at grades GS-13 and above. It would also affect the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) agency's budget and personnel compensation structure.  **Key Effect:** The bill would allow higher-grade Border Patrol employees to receive special overtime compensation for hours worked beyond standard schedules, a benefit currently limited to lower-grade personnel. This could increase compensation costs for CBP but would extend overtime pay eligibility to more senior agents in the organization."
