summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill would require federal agencies to adopt and implement the Artificial Intelligence Risk Management Framework (AI RMF) created by the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) when they develop, procure, or use artificial intelligence systems. The framework provides guidance on identifying, measuring, and managing risks associated with AI applications across government operations.  **Key Effects and Provisions:** The bill would apply to all federal agencies using AI technology, affecting their internal practices and procurement decisions. Agencies would be required to follow NIST's framework when implementing AI systems, which could cover applications ranging from administrative functions to decision-making processes. The framework itself addresses areas such as AI system performance, bias and fairness, security, transparency, and accountability.  **Implementation Details:** The bill establishes a standard approach across the federal government for managing AI risks. This requirement would likely involve agencies assessing their current AI use, adopting the NIST framework for new or existing systems, and documenting their compliance efforts. The specific timeline for implementation and any exceptions or exemptions would depend on the bill's detailed language."
