The bill would affect the general public by establishing official recognition for a month and day intended to focus attention on gun violence issues. It does not impose regulations, allocate funding, or create new government agencies or responsibilities.  The key provision is the expression of support for the dual designations. As a resolution rather than legislation with legal force, the bill serves primarily as a symbolic statement of congressional intent to recognize and raise awareness about gun violence during this period."
