summary: "# Bill Summary: School Social Work Week Designation  This bill proposes a congressional resolution to officially recognize the week beginning March 2, 2026, as \"School Social Work Week.\" The measure is a symbolic designation intended to raise awareness of the role and contributions of school social workers in the U.S. education system.  The designation would primarily affect school social workers and the organizations that represent them, as it provides national recognition for their profession. The week would also draw attention to the broader education community and policymakers to the work these professionals perform within schools.  Key provisions of the bill include establishing the specific week in 2026 for this recognition. As a resolution expressing support rather than legislation creating new requirements or funding, the bill carries symbolic rather than substantive legal weight. It does not create new programs, mandate actions, or allocate resources, but rather encourages awareness and appreciation of school social workers' contributions to student success and school safety."
