The bill would revoke the emergency declaration that authorized tariff increases, which would end the legal authority under which those tariffs were imposed.  The resolution would primarily affect businesses and consumers who import goods subject to the tariffs, as well as domestic producers in affected industries. If passed, it would require the removal or suspension of the tariffs that were implemented under the emergency declaration.  **Key provision:** The resolution directly terminates the emergency declaration itself, which would eliminate the legal basis for the tariffs imposed under that declaration.
