The bill acknowledges ACOG's role in the obstetrics and gynecology profession and its contributions to patient health through clinical practice, medical education, advocacy, and research activities.  The bill does not establish new laws, programs, or funding mechanisms. Instead, it formally recognizes a professional medical organization's milestone anniversary and its work in advancing obstetric and gynecologic care. This type of legislation is ceremonial in nature and serves to honor an organization's history and contributions to the medical field."
