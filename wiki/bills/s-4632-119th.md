summary: "# Bill Summary  This bill establishes procedures to address federal government funding lapses when Congress fails to pass regular appropriations legislation by the fiscal year deadline. Rather than allowing an immediate government shutdown, the bill creates a mechanism for continuing appropriations during the gap period. It would set rules for how federal agencies continue operations and spending when normal appropriations have not been enacted, establishing both the duration and scope of this continuing funding authority.  The bill also defines procedures and consequences that take effect if Congress does not pass appropriations legislation within a specified timeframe. These provisions would outline which government functions continue, at what funding levels, and for how long, as well as establish any penalties or operational restrictions during the lapse period. The measure affects all federal agencies and their employees, as well as beneficiaries of federal programs and services.  In essence, the bill aims to reduce uncertainty and disruption caused by appropriations lapses by creating an automatic mechanism and clear rules rather than relying on ad-hoc continuing resolutions or allowing sudden government shutdowns."
