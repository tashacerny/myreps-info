summary: "# Summary of School Social Work Week Designation Bill  This bill expresses congressional support for designating the week of March 2, 2025, as \"School Social Work Week.\" The measure is a resolution intended to recognize and honor the work performed by school social workers in the nation's schools.  The designation affects school social workers and the education community. School social workers provide support services to students, including counseling, crisis intervention, family outreach, and coordination with community resources. The week-long recognition is meant to acknowledge their contributions to student welfare and school functioning.  As a designation resolution, the bill carries no direct regulatory or budgetary requirements. It serves primarily as a symbolic statement of congressional recognition rather than creating new programs or policies. Such resolutions do not have the force of law but can raise public awareness about specific professions or observances."
