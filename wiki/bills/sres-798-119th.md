summary: "# Summary: Great Outdoors Month Resolution  This resolution designates June 2026 as \"Great Outdoors Month.\" The measure is a symbolic designation that recognizes and promotes outdoor recreation and activities during that month. As a resolution rather than legislation, it does not create new laws or authorize spending, but instead expresses the intent of Congress to honor outdoor activities and encourage public participation in outdoor pursuits.  The designation affects federal recognition and messaging during June 2026. It may be used to coordinate promotional activities, educational campaigns, and public awareness efforts related to outdoor recreation. The resolution could encourage participation in activities such as hiking, camping, fishing, parks visitation, and other outdoor pursuits. Federal agencies, states, and private organizations may use the designation to highlight outdoor recreation opportunities and environmental stewardship.  The measure has no direct fiscal impact or binding requirements on government operations or private entities. Its primary effect is to establish an official federal recognition of outdoor recreation during that specific month, similar to other designations Congress makes throughout the year for various causes, months, or observances."
