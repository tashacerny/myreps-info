summary: "# Summary of National Extreme Heat Awareness Week Resolution  This measure expresses congressional support for designating July 3–10, 2026, as \"National Extreme Heat Awareness Week.\" The resolution aims to establish a national observance focused on educating the public about the dangers of extreme heat and its various impacts on society. The week is intended to raise awareness among the general population about heat-related risks and mitigation strategies.  The resolution identifies several areas affected by extreme heat, including public safety, infrastructure, agriculture, and other sectors. By establishing this awareness week, the measure seeks to highlight how extreme heat events can threaten human health, damage physical infrastructure, reduce agricultural productivity, and create broader challenges for communities. The resolution does not appear to create new programs or allocate funding; rather, it designates a specific time period for national awareness and education efforts.  This type of resolution is largely symbolic and does not enforce requirements on individuals or organizations. It allows Congress to officially recognize and encourage awareness of extreme heat as a public concern during the designated week in 2026."
