summary: "# Summary of Mental Health Awareness Month Designation Bill  This bill expresses Congressional support for designating May 2026 as \"Mental Health Awareness Month.\" The measure is a symbolic resolution intended to recognize and promote awareness of mental health issues during that month. It does not create new laws or establish mandatory programs, but rather formally acknowledges the importance of mental health in the United States.  The bill affects the general public and organizations focused on mental health advocacy by providing a Congressional designation that can be used to promote mental health awareness activities and initiatives. The designation may encourage media coverage, public education campaigns, and community events related to mental health during May 2026. This type of designation allows government and nonprofit organizations to coordinate messaging around a shared theme.  Key provisions would include the official Congressional recognition of May 2026 as \"Mental Health Awareness Month.\" Beyond this designation, the bill is primarily a statement of support and does not establish new funding, regulatory requirements, or programmatic changes."
