summary: "# Summary of National Boys and Girls Club Week Recognition Bill  This bill proposes to officially recognize and celebrate National Boys and Girls Club Week in 2026. The legislation designates a specific week during 2026 as a national observance dedicated to honoring the Boys and Girls Clubs of America and the work they perform in communities across the country.  The bill affects the organization Boys and Girls Clubs of America, their local chapters, the youth members they serve, and communities where these clubs operate. It is a ceremonial measure that does not typically create new programs or allocate funding, but rather provides formal congressional recognition of the organization's contributions.  Key provisions would include establishing the official dates for the observance in 2026 and expressing congressional support for the Boys and Girls Clubs' mission of providing safe spaces, programs, and mentorship to young people. Such recognition bills are generally symbolic in nature, intended to raise awareness of the organization's work and encourage public engagement with local club activities."
