The bill would primarily affect biological research communities, including academic institutions, national laboratories, and potentially private research organizations. The Department of Energy would bear responsibility for establishing and maintaining this centralized data resource. Researchers in fields such as genomics, molecular biology, systems biology, and related disciplines would be among the primary users.  Key provisions would likely include creating infrastructure for data storage and management, establishing standards for data accessibility and interoperability, and potentially funding the ongoing operation of this resource.
